What You'll Learn

Understand Your Equity and Why It's Essential

Equity compensation comes with its own vocabulary, tax triggers, and strategic decisions. Here's what we'll cover.

📖

Speak the Language of Equity

ISOs, RSUs, ESPP, AMT, bargain element: we'll translate the terminology into plain English so you can clearly understand your grants, documents, and key decision points.

🧾

Anticipate the Tax Impact

Many costly surprises happen because employees don't realize when taxes are triggered. Learn where ordinary income, capital gains, and AMT can arise, and how proactive planning may help.

📈

Turn Knowledge Into Strategy

Understanding your equity is the first step. We'll show how these decisions connect to diversification, cash flow, and long-term wealth building so your compensation works harder for you.
